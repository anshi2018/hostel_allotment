Procedure for hostel allotment


 

 

» Registration.

» If you are a fresh candidate(name is not present in any of the list)then

→      All wing details are available at allotment status page, visit the page and select any wing.

→      If any vacant wing is present, you will be alloted that wing.

→      If you do not select any of the wing till last date, your name will be registered for waiting list.

» If your name has been already given in any list then:

→       If you want a vacant wing then if any vacant wing is present you will be allotted that wing.

→      Otherwise you will be given choices to choose a wing from available wings.

→      If you do not select any of the wing till last date, your name will be registered for waiting list.

» All candidates registered for waiting list will be alloted wing after the allotment of wings for other candidates.

» In waiting list, candidates will be allotted wings as per the availability of wings after allotment for other candidates.
